In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, Caplan Innovations LLC may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing your data

Caplan Innovations LLC will take reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

Caplan Innovations LLC will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers, which are managed and hosted by Heroku.

Caplan Innovations LLC will store all of your personal information and data separately from other users. With respect to other users of Caplan Innovations LLC’s services, only your account has the ability to access and use your information and data.

All payments made to Caplan Innovations LLC are handled by Stripe (Stripe, Inc.) which is compliant with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI-DSS) and Payment Application Data Security Standards (PA-DSS). Caplan Innovations LLC is compliant with the PCI Standards, since Caplan Innovations LLC implements the Stripe services in a manner that is compliant with Stripe’s standards. Caplan Innovations LLC does not store any of your payment data, which includes credit card number, expiration date, or any other “Sensitive Authentication Data” as defined by the PCI Standards (including CVC or CVV2), data at any time.
