Help

This is an Outlook Calendar Event template that can be reused and selected whenever you schedule a new appointment. Later, once you create your first template, you will be able to select a template from the list after "exporting the appointment to Outlook".

With every template there are "variables" you can use to have your templates get filled out even faster when you want to export an appointment via outlook. Variables allow you to fill in standard appointment information with the data you generated upon creating the appointment in @BaseController.APP_NAME .

Here are the variables you have access to:

  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_NAME - The prospect's name
  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_PHONE - The prospect's phone number
  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_EMAIL - The prospects email
  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_JOB_TITLE - The prospect's job title
  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_COMPANY - The prospect's company
  • @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_APPOINTMENT_DATE - The date of the appointment, formatted as "3/21/2017 at 3:00 PM"

For instance, to have the subject auto-fill to "Meeting with John Doe", set the subject to "Meeting with @CalendarTemplate.VARIABLE_PROSPECT_NAME"
